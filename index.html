Based in Nottinghamshire, UK. Started editing in May 2020 — self-taught, no shortcuts. Six years later the client base speaks for itself.

I offer both filming and post-production — stabilised 4K capture on location across the East Midlands, or working with footage you provide from anywhere. Everything is graded, sound designed and finished in-house.

Compact, discreet setup that works around your customers, not against them. No crew, no disruption, in and out fast.

Open to commercial commissions and part-time creative roles — remote or hybrid, while continuing freelance work.
